Brand In Use
Logo Variants

The "F" mark anchors the brand. Use the navy gradient as the default; switch to the gold mark only on dark navy surfaces where contrast is needed.

F

App Icon — Navy

Primary mark on a navy-to-blue gradient. Used for the launcher icon, splash screen, and login header. Minimum size 48 px.

F

Accent Mark — Gold

Gold variant reserved for dark backgrounds, badges, achievement icons, and active navigation states.

Logo Lockup & Clear Space

Always keep clear space around the logo equal to the height of the "F" mark. Never crowd it with text or other elements.

F
FBLA·Member
  • Minimum clear space = height of the mark on all sides.
  • Minimum app-icon size: 48 px (mobile launcher).
  • Corner radius: 22 px on icon, 12–16 px on UI cards.
  • Wordmark always set in Inter Black (900).
Color Do's & Don'ts
Do
  • Use Navy for app bars, headers & primary buttons.
  • Use Gold sparingly as an accent for CTAs & highlights.
  • Keep body text Ink Navy on light backgrounds.
  • Maintain AA contrast (4.5:1) for all text.
Don't
  • Don't place gold text on a white background (low contrast).
  • Don't use gold as a large background fill.
  • Don't introduce off-brand colors for buttons.
  • Don't stretch, recolor, or rotate the logo mark.
